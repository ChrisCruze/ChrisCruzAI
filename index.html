About Me

A seeker, builder, and connector who thrives at the intersection of tech, creativity, and human connection.

Born in Pleasantville, New York to Ecuadorian and Dominican parents, I've always been drawn to building bridges between different worlds. My journey has taken me from founding startups that scaled to 200+ people, to leading AI initiatives at J.P. Morgan, to exploring 15+ countries as a digital nomad.

I believe that structure creates freedom, and that the best technology enhances human potential rather than replacing it. This philosophy drives everything I build - from productivity systems to AI solutions to the comprehensive life management platform I'm developing called LifeOS.
