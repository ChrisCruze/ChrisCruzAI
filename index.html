About Me

I'm Chris Cruz — a full-stack builder, automation nerd, and AI strategist based in New York.

I've worked across both startups and corporate giants, helped scale a venture-backed company from 0 to 200 people, built van life rigs, and spoken at AI NYC. Now, I run a boutique AI agency helping founders and teams integrate GPT-powered workflows into how they work and build.

My journey started in Pleasantville, New York, took me through Minnesota, and brought me back to the city that never sleeps. Along the way, I've learned that the best solutions come from combining human creativity with smart automation.
